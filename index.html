How to buy TPDC

Step 1
Create wallet

Download metamask or the wallet of your choice for free from the App Store or Google Play Store. Desktop users please visit metamask.io to download the google chrome extension.

Step 2
Get some BNB

You have BNB in your wallet, which can be redeemed for $TPDC. If you don't have any BNB, you can buy it directly on metamask, transfer it from another wallet, or buy it on another exchange and have it sent to your wallet.

Step 3
Go to Pancakeswap

Connect to Pancakeswap. Visit https://pancakeswap.finance/ in google chrome or on your browser within the Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $TPDC token address into Uniswap, select TPDC, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for your wallet signature, sign it.

Step 4
Exchange BNB for $TPDC

Swap BNB for $TPDC. We operate zero tax, so you don't need to worry about buying with specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during market fluctuations.
